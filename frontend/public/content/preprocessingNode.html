If we feed that directly into a math algorithm, the computer will crash because it does not know how to multiply a blank space.

2.1 The "Strategy" Dropdown

If you choose to "Fill Missing Values", you must give the computer a strategy for what to fill those blanks/empty rows with.

  • 𝗨𝘀𝗲 𝗔𝘃𝗲𝗿𝗮𝗴𝗲: This is a popular statistical strategy. If you have a column tracking the "Age" of patients, and one patient's age is missing, the computer will calculate the average (mean) age of all the other patients and insert that number into the blank space. By relying on math and statistics, you keep your dataset intact without skewing the data too much.
